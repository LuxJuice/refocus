# refocus Dashboard Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Overview

refocus Dashboard ("refocus," "we," "our") is a personal home dashboard application that displays calendar events, weather, photos, and other household information on a dedicated display device. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data We Access

### Google Calendar Data
- We access your Google Calendar to display upcoming events on your dashboard
- Data accessed includes: event titles, times, dates, and calendar colors
- Access is read-only; we cannot modify your calendar

### Weather Data
- We use your configured location to fetch local weather information
- Location data is stored locally on your device

### Photos
- Photos you upload are stored locally on your dashboard device
- Photos are not transmitted to any external servers

### Household Information
- Names and preferences you configure are stored locally on your device

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:
- Display calendar events on your personal dashboard
- Show relevant weather information for your location
- Display your uploaded photos
- Personalize your dashboard experience

## Data Storage

- **All data is stored locally** on your refocus dashboard device
- OAuth tokens for Google services are stored locally and encrypted
- No personal data is transmitted to or stored on external servers
- No analytics or tracking data is collected

## Third-Party Services

### Google Calendar API
- We use Google's Calendar API to fetch your calendar events
- Your use of Google services is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- You can revoke refocus's access at any time via [Google Account Permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)

### Weather Services
- Weather data is fetched from third-party weather APIs
- Only your configured city/location is shared to retrieve forecasts

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties.**

Your data remains on your local device and is never transmitted to us or any other party.

## Data Retention

- Data remains on your device until you delete it
- Uninstalling refocus removes all locally stored data
- You can reset your dashboard at any time through the settings menu

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data (stored locally on your device)
- **Delete** your data at any time via device reset
- **Revoke** Google Calendar access via your Google Account settings
- **Disconnect** from any integrated services

## Security

- OAuth tokens are stored securely on your local device
- All communication with Google services uses encrypted HTTPS connections
- Your dashboard operates on your local home network

## Children's Privacy

refocus is designed for household use and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
